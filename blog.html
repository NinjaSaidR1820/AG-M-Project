Latte Art

Art made with coffee cream, known as latte art, is a visual expression created by pouring steamed milk over espresso coffee. Baristas use techniques such as pouring and drawing with simple tools to create intricate designs on the coffee's surface, such as hearts, leaves, or geometric shapes. This type of art not only adds aesthetics to the coffee but also showcases technical skill and creativity from the artist..

The Collection of Roasted Coffee Beans

The collection of roasted coffee beans involves gathering batches of coffee beans that have undergone the roasting process. Roasted beans are typically collected after they have cooled down sufficiently to be handled safely. This process ensures that the beans are ready for packaging, distribution, or immediate consumption. Roasted coffee beans are valued for their aroma, flavor profile, and suitability for brewing into coffee beverages.

Coffee Bean Production

Coffee bean production involves several key stages. It begins with the cultivation of coffee plants, which typically grow in tropical regions with specific climate and soil conditions. The plants produce coffee cherries, which contain the coffee beans. These cherries are harvested either by hand-picking or mechanical methods.

The Preparation of Espresso Coffee

The preparation of espresso coffee involves several key steps to achieve its distinctive flavor and texture. Firstly, fresh coffee beans are finely ground to a specific consistency. The ground coffee is then tightly packed into a portafilter, a metal basket with a handle.
